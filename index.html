Flower Care Tips

Keep your flowers looking fresh longer

Water Properly

Change water every 2-3 days and add flower food

Trim Stems

Cut stems at a 45-degree angle every few days

Avoid Direct Sun

Keep flowers in a cool spot away from direct sunlight and heat

Remove Foliage

Remove leaves below water line to prevent bacterial growth
